Marc's Moments & Experiences

CONTENT SUBJECT TO ANY CHANGE AT ANYTIME

Through your life you go through experiences, good ones and bad ones, some you want to share and some you want to keep for yourself. This is a website (as mentioned above, in construction) that I want to use with two main purposes:

  • Learn how to create content on-line, beginning with HTML, then CSS and hopefully some back-end in the near future, making it interactive
  • Share stuff: experiences, pictures, opinions, music... I have no idea what content to write yet, I guess it will come to my mind spontaneaously

One thing I want to be able to do is to make real content. By real I mean not just the good and happy sides of life we all see daily in social media. I want to normalize bad moods, experiences or moments.

Feelling bad, sad, or angry is completely normal, and fine. I believe our rejection to it is what keeps us fighting it. I guess you just gotta accept it and acknowledge it.


Easier saying it than doing it, of course.


By saying this I don't mean I want/will to share everything, or every mood I am in, but some times I might just make a statement of something that keeps me worried or in an uncomfortable state of mind. I also think (and that's perhaps the most powerful reason) that being vulnerable and showing yourself like this sometimes, is a proof of self-confidence and self-appreciation. You gotta act like it to become it, or so I hope.
